Declan Landy has been working as a professional musician for over 25 years, gigging throughout Ireland and Europe. Declan is qualified at Grade 8 in both Acoustic & Electric Guitar with the Registry Of Guitar Tutors, with accreditation from the University Of West London.

He has played to audiences in venues such as Dublin’s Olympia Theatre, Vicar St., The Button Factory and in famous London venues The 100 Club and Half Moon. In 2013 Declan’s song ‘Give A Little Love’ was chosen by legendary music producer Steve Lillywhite as part of the RTE TV show ‘The Hit’.

Declan has featured on radio on 2FM’s Gerry Ryan show, on Radio 1 with Marty Whelan, on Newstalk FM and on Radio Nova’s Colm & Lucy show. Declan also plays in the Pink Floyd tribute band “Visions Of Floyd”, playing theatre shows including two in The National Concert Hall with the RTE Concert Orchestra in 2019.
