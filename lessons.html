Guitar Lessons

Learn to play Acoustic or Electric Guitar with a full time qualified professional musician in the Palmerstown area. Guitar lessons are tailored to the age, background, skill level and musical taste of each student.

Lessons are on a one to one basis for 30 minutes, 45 minutes or 1 hour. Students will learn to play chords, scales, melodies, technique and of course songs, using guitar tablature or music notation, and each student may request specific songs they would like to play if they so wish. Zoom lessons are also available .

As a student you can prepare for graded exams with the Registry Of Guitar Tutors, gaining a recognised qualification from the University Of West London, or if you do not wish to take an exam the lessons will still help you to achieve your full potential as a guitarist by developing all aspects of guitar playing.

From beginners to advanced the goal is to foster good musicianship, build confidence and pass on a love of music to every student.
