What people are saying about PalmerstownGuitarLessons..

"My daughter and her friend have been taking guitar lessons with Declan for the last few years. They absolutely love the lessons and have learned so much, as well as picking up certificates from the University of West London. Even during the Covid lockdown the girls enjoyed their lessons via ‘Zoom’ which was fantastic. I would fully recommend Declan as a guitar teacher."

- Vera Carey, Palmerstown

"Declan is an excellent and very knowledgeable teacher of the guitar. Thanks to my many years studying the instrument under Declan, I am currently enrolled in a Bachelors Degree in Commercial Modern Music at the BIMM institute Dublin."

- Sam Dobbyn, Palmerstown

"Having tried many times over the years to learn to play guitar to no avail, I began lessons with Declan 18 months ago and cannot believe how well I am doing. Declan is an exceptional teacher, I have advanced more than I could have ever imagined . He is so patient and encouraging and makes the lessons fun. He has taught me everything - from picking notes to different playing techniques . I have learned so many songs and he is focused on teaching you what you want to learn. He explains the theory so well and makes it easy and interesting . I started on Acoustic guitar and have now bought an Electric one. He has taught me the importance of learning scales and practicing daily. He is a very skilled player and loves what he’s does. I would recommend Declan as a teacher to anyone who wants to learn from scratch or improve their skills. I look forward each week to the lessons and am delighted with the results."

- Yvonne Hanrahan , Palmerstown
